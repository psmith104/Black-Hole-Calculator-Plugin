The Black Hole Calculator computes relevant quantities about black holes in two systems of units. Add the inputs and press Calculate. Two calculator modes are available.

Standard Mode

Input: Black hole mass
Outputs: Category, Eddington luminosity, Eddington accretion rate, Event horizon radius, ISCO radius

Advanced Mode

Inputs: Black hole mass, Black hole seed mass
Outputs: Category, Eddington luminosity, Eddington accretion rate, Event horizon radius, ISCO radius, Formation time, Hawking temperature, Evaporation time, Surface area, Surface gravity
